About Me

Meet Clare

Welcome! I'm a culinary historian and speaker with a passion for the Georgian period. My journey began over forty years ago, sparked by a fascination with daily life of years past and, of all things, the humble meal.

I specialise in delivering engaging, informative, and entertaining talks and live cooking demonstrations that explore the kitchens, recipes, and dining etiquette of the past.

From grand feasts to simple fare, I aim to connect audiences with history in a way that is both tangible and delicious. I believe food is the ultimate gateway to understanding our ancestors.

Services

Talks & Demonstrations

I offer a range of services for historical societies, festivals, private groups, and corporate events. Each presentation is meticulously researched and delivered with passion.

Engaging History Talks

Lively, illustrated lectures on topics like "The Georgian Kitchen," "Dining with Jane Austen," and "The Great British Pork Pie: A History."

Live Cooking Demos

Watch, learn, and taste! I demonstrate historical recipes using period-appropriate techniques (with modern safety standards, of course).

Historical Consulting

Providing research and expertise for media, publications, and museums looking to ensure historical accuracy in their culinary portrayals.
